Canton,TX

Home of First Monday Trade Days

Canton is about 45-60 minutes from DFW and 45 minutes from Tyler, TX. It is a small town feel with multiple amenities including Walmart, Splash Kingdom, Yesterland Farms, and of course, Trade Days. Multiple small family owned businesses surround our neighborhood and you can find almost anything you are looking for during Trade Days. If you are looking to pursue a small start-up or want to make a few dollars one weekend a month then starting a booth at the world's largest flea market will put your name out there.

  • Splash Kingdom
